💡 How are these numbers calculated?
💡 Liquid Cash = Savings + Taxable Brokerage + Gold/Silver + Accumulated Cash Flow (AccCF). AccCF is the running total of all annual surpluses/deficits since 2027 — positive means extra undeployed cash; negative means you've spent more than earned. Net Worth excludes Retirement accounts (early-withdrawal penalties) in the "Excl. Home" figure.
