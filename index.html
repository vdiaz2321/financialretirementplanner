How are these numbers calculated?
Liquid Cash — Your Savings + Taxable Brokerage + Gold/Silver balances, plus any cash that has built up from yearly income exceeding expenses (or minus any shortfall if expenses exceed income). This represents money you can access without penalties.
Net Worth (Incl. Home) — Liquid Cash + Retirement accounts (TSP, Roth IRA) + projected home equity. Your total picture including everything you own.
Net Worth (Excl. Home) — Liquid Cash + Retirement accounts only, no home equity. Use this to gauge financial position independent of real estate.
Excl. Home & Retirement — Liquid Cash only. The most conservative view — what you could access today without selling your home or triggering early-withdrawal penalties on retirement accounts.
